SHIPPING AND CANCELLATION

When you place your order, we know that you want to receive the item as fast as possible. We will make that happen.

How long will the entire ordering process take from order submission to delivery?

We will do our best to deliver your product as fast as possible, depending on your location.

USA only:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-10 Business Days via USPS (expect a slight delay due to COVID)
  • Express Shipping: 3-5 Business Days via FedEx

After submitting your order, what's next?

  • Our staff will manually review your order, and it will be completed within 24 hours or faster. It includes checking all customer's info, shipping address, spelling errors, missing street number or other details, etc. Within this period, cancellation is still possible.
  • After the order review, your order will be fulfilled. It includes packaging, sorting, and dispatching. In this stage, NO CANCELLATION is accepted.
  • The delivery will be handled by USPS for standard delivery and FedEx for express shipping.
  • All orders are shipped with a tracking number, so you can track it every step of the way! Packages may face delays beyond our control such as postal delays.
